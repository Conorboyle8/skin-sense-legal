Privacy Policy for SkinSense

Last Updated: December 13, 2024

Introduction

SkinSense ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application SkinSense (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

Account Information:

  • Email address (if you create an account)
  • Name (if provided through Sign in with Apple)
  • Password (encrypted, never stored in plain text)

User-Generated Content:

  • Skincare routines you create
  • Products you add to your routines
  • Product ingredient information you scan and contribute
  • Product ratings or reviews (if applicable)

2. Information Collected Automatically

Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App version

Usage Information:

  • Features you use
  • Products you scan
  • Time spent in the app
  • Interaction with app features

Camera Access:

The App requests camera access to:

  • Scan product barcodes
  • Scan ingredient labels using OCR (Optical Character Recognition)

Photos/videos are NOT stored unless you explicitly choose to save them. Camera is only active when you're using the scanning feature.

3. Information from Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google):

We use Firebase for:

  • User authentication (Sign in with Apple, Email/Password)
  • Cloud data storage (your routines and products)
  • Analytics (app performance and crashes)

Barcode APIs:

  • We send barcode numbers to third-party services to retrieve product information
  • Only the barcode number is sent, no personal information

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App: Store your routines and products, sync data across devices, enable scanning
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage, fix bugs, develop new features
  • Communicate with you: Send important updates, respond to support requests, send notifications (if you opt-in)
  • Build community database: Use scanned ingredient data to help other users find information

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Share Your Information:

With Other Users:

  • Product and ingredient data you contribute becomes part of our shared database
  • Your email or personal information is NEVER shared with other users

With Service Providers:

  • Firebase/Google (for authentication and cloud storage)
  • Barcode lookup services (only barcode numbers, no personal data)

For Legal Reasons:

  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • In connection with a business transfer or merger

We DO NOT:

  • ❌ Sell your personal information
  • ❌ Share your email with third parties for marketing
  • ❌ Use your camera data for anything other than scanning
  • ❌ Store your photos or videos

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data is Stored:

  • Cloud storage: Firebase (Google Cloud, USA)
  • Local storage: On your device (encrypted)

Security Measures:

  • All data transmitted is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Firebase security rules protect your data
  • Regular security updates

Data Retention:

  • Account data: Kept until you delete your account
  • Anonymous usage data: Kept for 90 days
  • Product database contributions: Kept indefinitely to help community

Your Rights and Choices

You Have the Right to:

Access Your Data: View all your routines and products in the App

Update Your Data: Edit or update your routines and products anytime

Delete Your Data: Delete your account and all associated data (Go to Account → Delete Account). All your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Opt-Out: Decline camera permissions, disable analytics, stop receiving notifications

Data Portability: Request a copy of your data by contacting us

Children's Privacy

SkinSense is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Camera and Photo Library Permissions

Camera Permission:

  • Used ONLY for scanning barcodes and ingredient labels
  • Photos/videos are processed locally on your device
  • Nothing is uploaded or stored without your explicit action
  • No background access - camera only works when you actively use it

Sign in with Apple

If you use Sign in with Apple:

  • Apple may provide us with your name and email
  • You can choose to hide your email (Apple provides a private relay email)
  • We receive only the information you authorize Apple to share
  • Apple's privacy policy also applies: apple.com/legal/privacy

International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where Firebase/Google servers are located. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

If you are in the European Union (EU), we comply with GDPR and you have additional rights as described in "Your Rights and Choices" above.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Summary (TL;DR)

  • ✅ We collect: Email, routines, products, usage data, camera access
  • ✅ We use it for: App functionality, sync, improvements
  • ✅ We share: Product data with community, analytics with Firebase
  • ✅ We DON'T: Sell your data, store photos, share your email
  • ✅ You can: Delete account, export data, opt-out anytime
  • ✅ Camera: Only for scanning, nothing stored
